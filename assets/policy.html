Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Welcome to Cyber Shield, a cybersecurity awareness platform designed to help employees improve their knowledge and recognize security threats such as phishing, smishing, and malicious files. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when using the platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Employee name
  • Employee ID
  • Email address
  • Department (if applicable)

1.2 Usage Information

  • Login activity
  • Completed awareness activities (videos, articles, quizzes)
  • Awareness Level progress
  • Responses to phishing simulations (clicked / ignored / reported)

1.3 Technical Information

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • IP address
  • Time and date of platform access

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide access to the platform and authenticate users
  • Track user progress and awareness level
  • Improve training content and phishing simulations
  • Enhance overall cybersecurity awareness among employees
  • Generate reports for administrators to analyze awareness trends

3. Data Protection & Security

Cyber Shield implements:

  • Secure database storage
  • Role-based access controls (Admin / Employee)
  • Secure communication protocols (HTTPS)
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • File validation, signature (magic number) checking, and malware detection engines

4. Cookies & Tracking

The platform may use cookies to:

  • Maintain login sessions
  • Enhance platform performance
  • Personalize user experience

5. Your Rights

  • Access your stored personal data
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request account deletion (through the admin)

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Users will be notified of major changes to ensure transparency.
